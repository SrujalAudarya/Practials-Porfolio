About SSGMCE

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering, Shegaon was established in 1983 by Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan, Shegaon. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati.

The college is accredited by NAAC with 'A' Grade and has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in the region. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on academic excellence, SSGMCE provides a conducive environment for learning and growth.

Departments

SSGMCE offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines through its well-established departments.

Computer Science & Engineering

Established in 1986, the department offers B.Tech and M.Tech programs in Computer Science and Engineering.

  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Intake: 120)
  • M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Intake: 18)
  • State-of-the-art computer labs with latest software and hardware
  • Research areas: AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cloud Computing

Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

The department offers programs focused on electronics, communication systems, and signal processing.

  • B.Tech in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (Intake: 60)
  • M.Tech in Electronics & Telecommunication (Intake: 18)
  • Well-equipped laboratories for practical training
  • Research areas: VLSI, Embedded Systems, Wireless Communication

Mechanical Engineering

One of the oldest departments, offering comprehensive education in mechanical engineering.

  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Intake: 60)
  • M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Intake: 18)
  • Modern workshops and laboratories
  • Research areas: Thermal Engineering, CAD/CAM, Robotics

Electrical Engineering

The department focuses on power systems, electrical machines, and control systems.

  • B.Tech in Electrical Engineering (Intake: 60)
  • M.Tech in Electrical Power Systems (Intake: 18)
  • Modern laboratories with latest equipment
  • Research areas: Power Systems, Renewable Energy, Smart Grid
